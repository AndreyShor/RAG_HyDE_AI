CONCLUSIONS
In this Letter, we computed for the first time the scat-
tering amplitudes for the decay of a Higgs boson to three
partons as well as for the production of a Higgs boson
alongside another parton in the scattering of two par-
tons through third loop order in the generalized leading
color approximation. These amplitudes are essential in-
gredients to key collider observables studying the Higgs
boson at the forefront of precision phenomenology. Our
result is the first computation of a four-particle scatter-
ing amplitude at third loop order in QCD involving a
massive external particle and non-planar diagrams. We
attach our results in electronically readable files together
with the arXiv submission of this Letter.
