The two-loop Higgs impact factor

Abstract: In the HEFT, we consider the Regge limit of the two-loop amplitudes for Higgs
boson production in association with a jet, expanded to NNLL accuracy. We discuss the
issue of the Regge cuts versus poles in this context, and determine for the ﬁrst time the
Higgs impact factor at two-loop accuracy.
Keywords: perturbative QCD, Regge limit
